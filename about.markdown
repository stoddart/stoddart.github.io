Daniel Norman Stoddart is a platform engineer, former land surveyor, writer, editor, and Anglican clergyman from Media, Pennsylvania, USA. 

A descendant of lowland Scots from the River Tweed region and the people of East Anglia and Northern Ireland, he currently lives in the Negros Island Region (NIR) of the Philippines.
